What is Microvolunteering?

According to The Guardian, "Microvolunteering takes a simple idea – that people are more likely to volunteer their time in short and convenient, bite-sized chunks – and turns it into a new approach to community action. It offers volunteers a series of easy tasks that can be done anytime, anywhere, on your own terms." Micro-Action is dedicated to providing flexible, commitment free ways that you can help your community and make a difference in whatever way you can.

Mission Statement

Micro-Actions mission is to provide people with the opportunities to change the world.
